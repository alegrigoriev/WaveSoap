Process menu commands

The Process menu offers the following commands:

Sound cleanup Opens a submenu
Equalizer Allows to boost or attenuate frequency bands.
Filter Suppress lowest and/or highest frequencies in the sound
Normalize Volume Changes sound volume to bring the maximum to the specific level
DC offset Offsets the sound samples by specified number or calculates and compensates DC offset
Change Volume Changes the sound volume
Mute Zeroes the selected range, so it becomes silence
Invert Inverts sound samples in the selected range
Resample Changes sound sampling rate and/or tempo and pitch
Change Channels Changes sound from mono to stereo and back.
Swap channels Swaps left and right channels
Reverse Reverses the sound - makes it going backward
Stop Stops current lengthy operation

Sound cleanup submenu:

Click Removal Automatically finds and removes clicks from the sound
Turntable Noise Reduction Removes very low frequency static from the sound
White Noise Reduction Removes noise (hiss) from the sound
