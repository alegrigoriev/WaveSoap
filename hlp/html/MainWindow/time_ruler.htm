Time Ruler

Time ruler is drawn above the wave or FFT view and shows the current position in the file. The position can be shown in HH:MM:SS.ms format (hours, minutes, seconds, miliseconds), or seconds, or in samples. To change the sound format, right click the ruler, and select the desired menu item:

The context menu also allows to change time scale. Zoom In Full command sets 1:1 scale, to see the most details of the waveform.

On the left side of the time ruler, two buttons are shown:

The left button is for zooming in, the right one is for zooming out.
